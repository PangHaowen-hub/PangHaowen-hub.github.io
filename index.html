Short-Bio

I am a PhD candidate at the School of Integrated Circuits and Electronics, Beijing Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Dr. Chuyang Ye. I received my master degree from College of Medicine and Biological Information Engineering, Northeastern University, under the supervision of Dr. Shouliang Qi. I received my bachelor degree from College of Mathematics and Information Science, Hebei University. My research interests lie predominately in the area of medical image analysis and deep learning.
