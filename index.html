Short-Bio

I am a PhD student at Beijing Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Dr. Chuyang Ye.
I received master degree from Northeastern University (Shenyang, China), under the supervision of Dr. Shouliang Qi.
I received bachelor degree from College of Mathematics and Information Science, Hebei University.
My research interests lie predominately in the area of medical image analysis and deep learning.
